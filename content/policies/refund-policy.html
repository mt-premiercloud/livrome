If the book doesn't move you, we'll replace it or refund you. Your choice. No fine print, no return shipping fees.

We only ship books we'd be proud to keep ourselves. But printing is a physical process and every once in a while something goes wrong — a skewed trim, a dull cover, a color drift. When that happens, we make it right.

The promise

Within 30 days of delivery, if your book has:

  • A print or binding defect
  • A color or clarity issue you didn't anticipate from the preview
  • Damage from shipping
  • Or just — it's not what you hoped for

…write to us at hello@livrome.com with your order number and (if the book is physically flawed) one or two photos of the issue. We'll reply within one business day with your choice of:

  • Replacement — we reprint and ship a new copy at our cost. The original is yours to keep.
  • Full refund — we refund the order (book + tax + shipping) to your original payment method. No return needed.

What's not covered

We can't refund or replace books that are:

  • Damaged after delivery (water, crayons, toddler physics — we understand, but we can't cover these)
  • Reported more than 30 days after delivery
  • Requested to change the story, character, or illustration content after the preview was confirmed (the moment you click "Order the book" is the snapshot we print)

If you spot something you want to change during the preview flow, you can regenerate or edit pages freely — the preview is infinite and free until you confirm.

Cancellations

Once printing starts (usually within 24 hours of your order), we can't cancel. Before then, email us and we'll stop the print and refund you.

Damaged in transit

If the box arrives crushed, torn, or wet — take a photo before opening, and email it to us. We'll reship at our cost.

Address errors

If a book returns to us because of an incorrect address, we'll email you and re-ship to a corrected address at no charge (once). Subsequent re-ships will incur the shipping fee.

How long refunds take

Once approved, refunds appear on your original card within 5–10 business days (depending on your bank).

Contact

hello@livrome.com — we reply within one business day, in French or English.
